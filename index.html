About Me

Computer Science Student

I am a dedicated and aspiring computer science student currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science Honours in Computer Science with a specialization in Software Engineering at the University of Windsor. Proficient in a range of programming languages, including Java, C, and Python, I have a strong foundation in both software development and system programming. Additionally, I have experience with frameworks such as Java3D, Django, and Jenkins, as well as web development technologies like HTML and CSS. In my academic journey, I have been actively engaged in various projects and coursework, including creating interactive games using Java and Java3D. I have also taken on leadership roles as a Scrum Master, where I demonstrated effective project management skills and fostered a collaborative team environment. Outside of academics, I have gained valuable experience as a web design intern, collaborating with a team to develop user-friendly websites, and as a team member at Tim Hortons, where I honed my communication and teamwork skills in a fast-paced environment. Furthermore, my role as a Customer Fulfillment Associate at Walmart has allowed me to improve efficiency and organization while ensuring excellent customer service. I have also volunteered for event management teams and organizations, further developing my interpersonal and public speaking skills. Overall, I am a motivated and adaptable individual with a passion for technology and a strong commitment to continuous learning and improvement. I am excited to contribute my skills and knowledge to future endeavors in the field of computer science and software engineering.
